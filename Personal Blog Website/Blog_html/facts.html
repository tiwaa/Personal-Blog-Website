At a population level, healthy

living refers to the practices of

population group that are

consistent with supporting,

improving, maintaining and/or

enhancing health.


As it applies to individuals, healthy living is the practice of health enhancing behaviours, or put simply,living in healthy ways.

Tips:
  1. Eat three healthy meals a day.
  2. The bulk of food consumed must consists of healthy foods such as fruits, vegetables,whole grains etc.
  3. Avoid heavy meals in the summer months, especially during hot days.
  4. Avoid eating raw or uncooked meats of any type.
  5. Avoid self medication, rather see a doctor.
